Healthie released three major features designed to reduce administrative burden on healthcare providers. Ambient Scribe extends AI-powered documentation to in-person and phone sessions, Client Metrics brings patient outcome tracking directly into the charting interface, and Client-level Billing Type assignment streamlines payment workflows by eliminating manual billing overrides.
